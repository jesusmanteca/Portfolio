About Jesus

Jesus was born in Guadalajara, in the heart of Mexico, surrounded by a large family, delicious food, and amazing culture. But only ten years later, his family moved to the United States, just outside of Chicago. Although the move was rough, facing a new country, a 'foreign' language, new people, and new climate, it would prepare him to adapt to a life full of change. Except high school, he never attended a school longer than two years, even in college. He began college in Marquette University to study communications but after two years, transferred to DePaul University where he earned a degree in psychology of human development.

After college, he studied health sciences at Loyola University, earning a post baccalaureate certificate and began working as a medical assistant in busy private practice at Northwestern Medicine. He soon became the managing director of the practice but found someting was missing. He was more interested in why the patients wouldn't take their medicine than what the medicine did for them. Figuring out why people act the way they do lead him to enter the field of advertising.

He began my new career as a planner and strategist in a small multicultural ad agency, Elemento L2. His career subsequently took him to client side working with V&V Supremo, an award-winning Hispanic Midwestern Cheese company. After growing the company's social media presence through paid media, community managing, and strategy, he began to work with DDB in Chicago on national accounts like Miller Lite, Wyndham Hotels, AT&T, and ConAgra, among many other brands. He also became a community manager for the renowned agency.

Two years later, he put my career on hold and along with his wife, they set off to travel. First to the Pacific Northwest and then to the Southwest in Arizona.
